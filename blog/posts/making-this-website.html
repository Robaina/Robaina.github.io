Making this website

20 August, 2019

I have been wanting to make my website since I started my Ph.D. I knew it was something important to have, but I just couldn't find the time to do it. Now that I have more time, I have decided to go for it! I could have easily built my website using some content management system, or I could have employed some available website template, or even use one of the several available JavaScript libraries to help me do it. However, I wanted to build a website from scratch, that is, using pure HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. On the one hand, I wanted to learn more about web development stuff during the process. On the other hand, I enjoy coding, and building a website from scratch, with full freedom to do whatever I wanted, seemed like a fun thing to do! Anyway, I thought I could share some tips I wish I would have told me when I started this project. So, here we go.

1. Keep your project well organized

You are going to generate a large quantity of files. This includes naming your folders in such a way that urls are human-readable. This way your address will be much easier to remember.
